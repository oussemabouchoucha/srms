About the Institute

The Higher Institute of Technological Studies of Kélibia (Arabic: المعهد العالي للدراسات التكنولوجية بقليبية), or ISET-Kélibia, is a Tunisian institution of higher education founded in 2012 with the aim of training graduates in applied bachelor's degree programs in the framework of the LMD reform. It has three departments: Department of Information Technology Department of Process Engineering Department of Marine Engineering The institution is located in Kélibia in the governorate of Nabeul.
